How it all began...
STEP 1
The one where it all began The one that kicked off a classic. Monica's old friend Rachel decides to move in with her after leaving her fiance. Meanwhile Ross takes an apartment in Monica's building to be closer to his friends, Chandler and Joey.
STEP 2
Season three… WE WERE ON A BREAK! The third season starts out with Ross and Rachel dating. Though Ross and Rachel have an off-and-on relationship throughout the series, we get to see them truly “dating” during the last season and the beginning of this one.
STEP 3
The season where Ross says:"Rachel" Now it’s time for the moment of truth, who did Ross pick? Shockingly, he picked Rachel. I’ll give you a second to react to that news. Where some fans, were elated at this news, it was a short lived rekindling of their relationship.
